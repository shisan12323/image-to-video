# Privacy Policy for AI Garden Design

## Introduction

Welcome to AI Garden Design, the **AI-powered garden visualization platform** that transforms your outdoor spaces using artificial intelligence. At AI Garden Design, your privacy is of utmost importance to us, and this privacy policy outlines how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you use our garden design services.

## Information Collection and Use

We collect the following types of data to provide you with a better experience while using AI Garden Design:

1. **Account Information**

   - **What We Collect**: This includes your name, email address, and any other information you provide when creating an account.
   - **Purpose**: To manage your account, provide customer support, and deliver personalized garden design services.

2. **Garden Design Data**

   - **What We Collect**: Images of your garden or outdoor space that you upload for AI transformation, design preferences, style selections, and generated design results.
   - **Purpose**: To provide AI-powered garden design services, improve our algorithms, and maintain your design history.

3. **Usage Details**

   - **What We Collect**: Information about how you use AI Garden Design, including your interactions, features accessed, design requests, and usage frequency.
   - **Purpose**: To analyze user engagement, improve our AI models, and enhance our garden design services.

4. **Device Information**

   - **What We Collect**: Data regarding the devices you use to access AI Garden Design, such as device type, operating system, and browser type.
   - **Purpose**: To optimize our services for different devices and ensure compatibility across platforms.

5. **Cookies**

   - **What We Collect**: Small data files placed on your device that help us track user preferences and improve user experience.
   - **Purpose**: To enhance the functionality of our services, personalize your garden design experience, and maintain session information.

6. **Payment and Billing Information**
   - **What We Collect**: Information regarding your payment methods, such as credit card number, billing address, and any details required for transaction processing.
   - **Purpose**: To facilitate billing and payment processing for our subscription plans and credit purchases.

7. **Credits and Usage Tracking**
   - **What We Collect**: Information about your credit balance, usage history, and design generation activity.
   - **Purpose**: To manage your account credits, track service usage, and provide accurate billing.

## Data Storage and Security

We take the security of your personal information and garden images seriously. Your data is stored securely using industry-standard encryption and security practices. We implement multiple layers of security measures including:

- **Encryption**: All data transmission and storage is encrypted using industry-standard protocols
- **Access Controls**: Strict access controls limit who can view your personal information and garden images
- **Secure Infrastructure**: We use secure cloud infrastructure with regular security audits
- **Image Protection**: Your uploaded garden images are stored securely and are not shared with unauthorized parties

However, please be aware that no method of transmission over the Internet, or method of electronic storage, is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

## AI and Image Processing

Your uploaded garden images are processed by our AI algorithms to generate design transformations. Please note:

- Images are processed securely and temporarily cached for performance optimization
- We may use anonymized design data to improve our AI models and services
- Generated designs remain your intellectual property
- We do not use your images for marketing purposes without explicit consent

## Information Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your personal information or garden images to outside parties, except in the following circumstances:

- To comply with legal obligations or respond to lawful requests from public authorities
- To protect our rights, property, or safety, or that of our users or others
- To provide services through trusted third-party partners (such as payment processors, AI service providers) who assist us in operating our platform, subject to strict confidentiality agreements
- With your explicit consent for specific purposes (such as showcasing your design in our gallery)

## Data Retention

We retain your personal information and garden designs for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide you services. You may request deletion of your account and associated data at any time by contacting us.

## Your Rights

You have the right to:
- Access your personal information and garden design data
- Request correction of inaccurate information
- Request deletion of your data (subject to legal retention requirements)
- Download your garden designs and account data
- Opt-out of marketing communications
